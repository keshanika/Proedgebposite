Services

Six services. One standard: more than what AI can do alone.

Software can process a transaction. It can't decide what an exception means for your business, or stand behind the number when someone asks why. Every service below includes that layer — a qualified accountant's judgment — as standard, not an upsell.

01 / Bookkeeping

Bookkeeping & Accounts Management

Day-to-day transaction recording, categorization, and ledger maintenance, kept accurate and audit-ready month after month.

More than AI

Software can miscategorize a one-off transaction and never notice. We catch the pattern behind it and fix the root cause, not just the entry.

02 / AP & AR

Accounts Payable & Receivable

Invoice processing, vendor payments, collections, and aging management — kept current and disputes handled directly.

More than AI

An automated matching tool flags a mismatched invoice and stops. We know your vendor terms well enough to resolve it same-day.

03 / Payroll

Payroll Processing

Accurate, on-time payroll runs including contractor payments, deductions, and multi-state considerations.

More than AI

A worker reclassification or a new state filing requirement doesn't fit a template — it takes someone who understands the compliance behind it.

04 / Reporting

Financial Reporting & Analysis

Monthly management reports, variance analysis, and dashboards built around the decisions you actually need to make.

More than AI

A generated report tells you what happened. We tell you why it happened and what it means for your next decision.

05 / Close

Month-End & Year-End Close

A structured, documented close process with reconciliations, accruals, and adjusting entries handled on schedule.

More than AI

Every close ends with a qualified accountant's sign-off — a name attached to the numbers, not just a completed checklist.

06 / Advisory

Compliance & Advisory Support

Practical guidance on financial process, internal controls, and compliance questions as your business grows.

More than AI

Compliance rules shift by state, industry, and situation. That kind of judgment call isn't something a tool can be accountable for — we are.

The short version

Automation handles the volume. We handle what matters.

Every ProEdge engagement uses modern accounting tools to move fast on routine work — then puts a CGMA/CIMA-qualified accountant on everything that requires judgment, context, or accountability. That's the standard across all six services, on every engagement, every month.
