About ProEdge BPO

Outsourcing that still puts a qualified accountant behind every number.

ProEdge BPO exists because too much of the F&A outsourcing market has quietly become "run it through software and hope." We built the opposite: a firm where automation supports the work, but a qualified professional still owns every outcome.

The founder

Led by 15+ years in finance and accounting.

ProEdge BPO is led by a CGMA/CIMA-qualified finance professional with roughly fifteen years of hands-on experience across accounting, financial reporting, and management functions. That background shapes how the firm works: every engagement is treated as something a real accountant is accountable for, not a workflow that runs itself.

The firm was built specifically to serve US-based businesses that want the cost efficiency of outsourcing without giving up the judgment and accountability of an in-house finance professional.

How we work

Technology-assisted. Human-led. In that order.

Step 01

Tools do the heavy lifting

We use modern accounting software and automation to handle data entry, categorization, and first-pass reconciliation quickly and consistently.

Step 02

A qualified accountant reviews everything

Every close, every reconciliation, and every exception is reviewed by someone with the training to know what "correct" actually looks like for your business.

Step 03

Judgment gets documented

When a decision requires interpretation — a reclassification, a compliance question, a client-specific exception — we record the reasoning, not just the result.

Step 04

You always know who to ask

No black box. If you have a question about a number, there's a named professional who can answer it.

What we believe

Our values

01 Judgment over templates
